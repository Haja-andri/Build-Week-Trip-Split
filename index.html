Frequents Questions

What is Trip-Split ?

Trip-Split is a shared list of transactions which lets you and your friends balance your joint expenditure, such as during a shared activity, on holiday or in a house share.

How does Trip-Split work ?

Everyone enters his/her expenses, Trip-Split calculates the balance of each participant and proposes the best way to settle up between group members.

What is the origin of the name "Trip-Split"?

The method with which people pay back their friends happens in a sort of triangular manner. For example, if John owes Katie 10 euros and Katie owes Fred 10 euros, then John can pay Fred directly, and everyone is even. Tricount combines the words triangular account.

Do I have to login to use the app ?

No, the only thing you need to use a tricount is the unique link to it (usually sent by email). The only reason tricount asks "who you are" in a group is to calculate your total & ease the input of new expenses.

Why isn't it possible to use multiple currencies in a Trip-Split?

This functionality isn't currently available. We advise our users to create one tricount per currency - eg. "road trip $" and "road trip EUR".
